Ambulance Performance

Ambulance Victoria has a target of responding to Code 1 incidents within 15 minutes at least 90% of the time in urban areas and 85% state wide. Code 1 incidents are those where patients require urgent paramedic assistance.

Western Australia's ambulance expenditure has remained relatively flat over the last ten years. Tasmanian spending has dramtically increased in the last five years.
